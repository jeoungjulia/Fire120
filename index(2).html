The Strategy

Simple rules.
Extraordinary discipline.

The Fire120 strategy is built on three pillars — fundamental quality, technical timing, and perpetual compounding. No complexity for its own sake. Only what works.

01
Rule of 40. Only invest in companies where Revenue Growth % + Profit Margin % exceeds 40. Below 40, watch. Above 40, own.
02
BB + RSI Swing Trading. Bollinger Bands + RSI below 35 at the lower band = buy signal. Only on stocks you are happy to own forever.
03
No Covered Calls on Winners. Hard lesson learned. CSP income only. Never cap the upside on stocks held for 120% gains.
04
Perpetual Reinvestment. Every dividend buys more MSTW and YETH on dips. The machine compounds itself. Never interrupt it.
